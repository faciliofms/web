About Facilio

Redefining facility management through innovation, sustainability, and operational excellence

OUR STORY

Building Smarter Facilities Since 2008

Founded in Bangalore with a vision to transform facility management, Facilio has grown from a local service provider to a national leader in integrated facility solutions. Our journey began with a simple premise: facilities should work smarter, not harder.

Today, we manage over 50 million square feet of commercial space across India, combining human expertise with cutting-edge technology to deliver unparalleled service quality and operational efficiency.
