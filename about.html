About Facilio

Sustainable, technology-driven facility management solutions from Kerala

OUR COMPANY

Kerala's Emerging Facility Management Leader

Facilio Facility Management Services Pvt. Ltd. is a Thiruvananthapuram-based startup offering end-to-end integrated facility management solutions. Incorporated on July 16, 2025 and registered with the Ministry of Corporate Affairs, India, we are on a mission to transform facility management through sustainable practices and advanced technologies.

Our solutions focus on optimizing operational efficiency through smart energy management systems that significantly reduce power consumption across key facilities components including elevators, HVAC systems, STPs, RO plants, and lighting systems.
